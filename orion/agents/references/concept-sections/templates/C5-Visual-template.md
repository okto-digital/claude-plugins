## Field Notes

- Direction, not specification: "warm and approachable" not "#FF6B35 primary"
- Every direction should be grounded in research evidence (persona expectations, competitor gaps, UX benchmarks)
- References should be real URLs from research with specific notes on what to take from each
- The visual direction is about differentiation — what makes this brand recognisable vs competitors who look similar
